Return Policy

At VOLTIC TECHNOLOGY PTE. LTD., we strive to ensure customer satisfaction with every purchase. If you are not completely satisfied with your product, you may request a return or exchange under the following conditions:

  • Returns are accepted within 14 days of receipt of your order.
  • The product must be unused, in its original packaging, and in the same condition as received.
  • Proof of purchase is required for all returns and exchanges.
  • Some items (such as custom orders or software licenses) may not be eligible for return. Please check with our support team for details.
How to Return an Item
  1. Contact our support team at info@volticinnovation.com with your order details and reason for return.
  2. We will provide instructions for returning your product.
  3. Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you regarding the approval or rejection of your refund.

Refunds will be processed to your original method of payment within 7-10 business days after approval.

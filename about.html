About Voltic Technologies technologys

So, Voltic Technologies has rapidly become a key player in the regional technology distribution market. The company was founded to assist import, export, and retail businesses by overcoming challenges associated with procuring genuine technology samples and facilitating small-scale production.

The company's 130,000 square meters of state-of-the-art warehouses are a testament to its growth and capability, stocking over 560,000 SKUs from 2,600 manufacturers. Voltic Technologies's extensive inventory allows it to offer a wide in-stock selection of components at competitive prices, catering to the diverse needs of its clientele. The company's in-house developed warehouse management system has streamlined order processing, with the ability to complete orders from parts picked to sorting in as little as four hours.

At the heart of Voltic Technologies's operations is a commitment to quality. With professional laboratories and equipped quality control teams, Voltic Technologies has formed a scientific and reliable quality management system. Voltic Technologies's dedication to excellence is further demonstrated by ISO 9001:2015 certification, which reflects an ongoing commitment to maintaining high standards.

Voltic Technologies's Quotes Team is a specialized unit designed to assist customers in sourcing technology components at competitive prices from a global network of suppliers. This service is particularly beneficial for customers seeking out-of-stock, obsolete, or end-of-life parts, or those looking to reduce procurement costs.

Global shipping is another strength of Voltic Technologies, with the company shipping to most countries worldwide. It offers a range of shipping options, including express delivery for urgent requests and economic shipping methods for non-urgent small orders. Voltic Technologies also provides a variety of payment methods, including PayPal, credit/debit cards, and wire transfers, with the flexibility of 100% prepaid terms and conditional NET terms.

Customer Service & After-sales

Customer service at Voltic Technologies is personalized, with a dedicated representative assigned to assist with order placement or resolving order issues. Technical support is also available from 09:30 to 18:30, Monday to Friday (GMT+8), ensuring that customers receive timely assistance with any issues they may encounter.

Voltic Technologies provides comprehensive after-sales service, offering a 30-day warranty against quality issues. Please note that non-cancelable/non-returnable products are excluded from returns. Additionally, the company offers its expertise in the production of premium PCB/PCBA prototypes and batch orders. Value-added services, including custom cable assemblies, front panel customization, and re-reeling services for orders with quantities less than a full reel, demonstrate Voltic Technologies's dedication to meeting the unique requirements of its customers. Voltic Technologies technologys maintains a customer-centric approach and a relentless pursuit of innovation, positioning itself as a reliable partner for technology component sourcing and manufacturing needs.
